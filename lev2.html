What do I need to know to pass JLPT N4?

N4 is very very similar to N5 but a bit tougher. First of all it is slightly longer than the N5 exam, where the N5 was 1hour 45mins, N5 is 2hours 5mins. The exam is split into 3 multiple choice papers covering vocabulary, grammar, reading and listening. There are NO speaking exams in the JLPT, and N4 onwards doesn’t have a spelling section which N5 does have.
  • Vocabulary knowledge covers:Kanji reading, the kanji for words in hiragana, using the correct word in context, and paraphrases, and usage in sentences.
  • Grammar knowledge covers:Grammar forms, sentence composition, text grammar.
  • Reading knowledge covers:Comprehension/understanding of short and mid-length paragraphs, and retrieving information.
  • Listening covers: task based understanding, key point understanding, verbal expressions and quick responses.

Grammer:

Grammar – You need to know grammar forms for verbs, adjectives and particles. You can see a list/guide to the grammar on the wikipedia page for JLPT. I strongly recommend Tae Kim’s Grammar Guide where he doesn’t teach grammar by JLPT level, but in a logical way mixing verbs, adjectives and particles together so you’re not cramming the same kind of grammar into your brain. This helps as it also allows you to learn what you previously learnt when you learn something new. Again, if you’re a Memrise fan someone’s putting together his guide on Memrise for people to practice: Japanese

Reading:

Reading will come last and be a good way to practice everything you’ve learnt. The best way to practice this is actually through practice questions, which I’ve put some links to below. If you’re worried about this JLPT Bootcamp has a good walk through of the layout of this part of the test. Recommended to make the reading practice day with japanese manga,comics,and some news.

Listening:

Listening – Listening can be tough if you don’t expose yourself to Japanese audio or speaking. I have more suggestions on Passing JLPT N5 N4 Listening. But I suggest working with a Japanese teacher to get used to speaking and listening. Then do some practice listening questions, which you can find on youtube and JapaneseTest4You.

Things to Follow to crack the Exam:

Build your vocabulary with essential words and phrases used in everyday conversations.

Repeat each phrase aloud.

Practice with a partner or record yourself to check pronunciation.

Tips for JLPT N4 Preparation

  • Practice Hiragana and Katakana thoroughly as they form the basis of Japanese writing.
  • Focus on learning basic vocabulary and kanji characters commonly used in everyday conversations.
  • Master basic grammar points such as sentence structure, verb conjugation, and particle usage.
  • Practice listening to Japanese audio materials to improve comprehension and pronunciation.
  • Take mock exams to familiarize yourself with the format and structure of the JLPT N5 exam.

Sample Quiz for JLPT N4

  1. Passage 1:

    新しいレストランがオープンしました。ここは日本料理のレストランです。メニューには寿司や刺身、天ぷらなどがあります。店内は清潔で、落ち着いた雰囲気です。また、ランチタイムにはお得なセットメニューもあります。ぜひ一度、お越しください。

    Questions:

    • What kind of restaurant recently opened?
    • What dishes are available on the menu?
    • What is mentioned about the atmosphere inside the restaurant?
    • What special offer is available during lunchtime?

    Answers:
    • 日本料理のレストラン
    • 寿司、刺身、天ぷら
    • 清潔で落ち着いた雰囲気
    • ランチタイムにお得なセットメニュー

  2. Passage 2:

    田中さんは大学生です。彼は毎日バスで学校に通っています。学校の近くにはコンビニがあります。彼は授業の後、友達と一緒にコンビニでお菓子を買っています。そして、夕食は家で母親と一緒に食べます。

    Questions:

    • What does Tanaka do for a living?
    • How does he usually go to school?
    • Where is the convenience store located?
    • What does he do with his friends after class?
    • Who does he have dinner with?

    Answers:
    • 大学生
    • バス
    • 学校の近く
    • 友達と一緒にコンビニでお菓子を買う
    • 母親と

  3. Passage 3:

    今週末、公園でバーベキューパーティーを開催します。参加希望者は事前に参加表明をしてください。持ち物には飲み物、お肉、野菜、そしてマットなどが含まれます。時間は午後１時から４時までです。楽しい時間を一緒に過ごしましょう！

    Questions:

    • Where will the barbecue party take place?
    • What should participants do before attending?
    • What items should they bring?
    • What is the duration of the event?

    Answers:
    • 公園
    • 参加表明をする
    • 飲み物、お肉、野菜、マット
    • 午後１時から４時
