Welcome to Level 1 Japanese!

Konnichiwa! Welcome to your first step in learning Japanese! In Level 1, you'll be introduced to the basics of the Japanese language, including essential vocabulary, grammar structures, and cultural insights.

Step 1: Greetings

In this lesson, you'll learn how to greet others in Japanese. Mastering greetings is essential for building connections and showing respect in Japanese culture.

Key Phrases:
  • こんにちは (Konnichiwa) - Hello (used during the day)
  • こんばんは (Konbanwa) - Good evening
  • おはようございます (Ohayou gozaimasu) - Good morning

Practice:

Repeat each phrase aloud.

Practice with a partner or record yourself to check pronunciation.

Consistency is the Key

Step 2: Introducing Yourself

Now, let's learn how to introduce yourself in Japanese. This will help you start conversations and make new friends!
Phrases:
  • わたしは [Your Name] です。 (Watashi wa [Your Name] desu.) - I am [Your Name].
  • どうぞよろしくおねがいします。 (Douzo yoroshiku onegaishimasu.) - Nice to meet you.

Practice:

  • Practice introducing yourself in front of a mirror.
  • Role-play with a friend, taking turns being the speaker and the listener.

Things to Follow to crack the Exam:

Build your vocabulary with essential words and phrases used in everyday conversations.

Repeat each phrase aloud.

Practice with a partner or record yourself to check pronunciation.

Tips for JLPT N5 Preparation

  • Practice Hiragana and Katakana thoroughly as they form the basis of Japanese writing.
  • Focus on learning basic vocabulary and kanji characters commonly used in everyday conversations.
  • Master basic grammar points such as sentence structure, verb conjugation, and particle usage.
  • Practice listening to Japanese audio materials to improve comprehension and pronunciation.
  • Take mock exams to familiarize yourself with the format and structure of the JLPT N5 exam.
